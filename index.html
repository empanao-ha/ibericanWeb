Founded December 29th, 2024, operating since 2025. Led by an experienced global team of directors, Iberican Airlines is one of the biggest Robloxian spanish airlines. The objective of this innovator operator bases on giving the best choice for the best price to the customers, the idea has brought success to the airline making it one of the powerfuliest operators on the industry of Roblox Aviation, we count with powerful allieds partners with the IbericanSky Group of the industry: Iberia Roblox and Vueling Roblox. The name of Iberican comes from the formation of the word of the Iberian peninsule in Spanish and the ending ''-can'' in English, remarking the 2 offical languages of our 2 airports that are our base of operations. Our main base of operations is the Barcelona International Airport, Josep Tarradellas, the IberianSky Group owns today: Iberican Airlines, Iberican Express & Iberican Asia. Our objective is clear: Bringing the better solutions to travel around the Robloxian globe for cheap prices and the best quality possible, giving reliable welcoming aircraft experiences and expanding the ways that more players of Roblox can visit detailed models of our destinations and departures in our flights.
